This high-fidelity wireframe is created for the purpose of helping Hospitals to have a systematic way of viewing Patients, Rooms, Treatment and Staff in one single webpage. This Dashboard shows the total number of patient, the availability of the hospital rooms and staffs to ensure that patients are being well assisted by the hospital staffs. The availability of the Treatment is also shown in the dashboard to easily change the availability of the treatment using the toggle button.
